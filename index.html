Anime- One of my loves

Posted on 2019-04-04

With me since my childhood

Anime has been one of my go-to media of entertainment since a very young age!
My first anime was Digimon! A great anime that introduced me to the amazing world where the impossible, was possible!
It has become more of a hobby and a thing that’s extremely dear to me as I have grown up.
Not a single day passes by when I don’t watch some of my favourite episodes from any given anime! They have been extremely inspiring, and I really love them!

My favourite!

If I had to choose one, my favourite anime series of all times has to be Naruto!
Naruto and Naruto shippuden have been absolute favourites.
Packed with life lessons and a great deal of emotions! Covering every aspect of human life, it taught me how fighting for your dreams given any condition could lead to greater merit!
It has to be one of the most awesome things I came across in my life!

A list of my favourites!

My other favourites include Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood, Yuri!!! On Ice, Haikyu!!, Kuroko no Basuke, Code Geass, Fairy Tail, Death Note, Uchuu Kyoudai, Gintama and digimon!
All these are worth watching and definitely provide me with a lot of excitement!
As I mentioned in my previous blog post, my great desire to visit Japan definitely comes from these amazing shows!
They have been amazing and I really love them!
New seasons for Haikyuu and a movie for Yuri!!! on Ice are coming up and I couldn’t be more excited!
It’s just so so amazing!
